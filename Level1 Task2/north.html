Paneer Tikka

Marinated cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) skewered and grilled, often served with mint chutney.


Tandoori Chicken

Chicken marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then roasted in a tandoor (clay oven), giving it a smoky flavor.



Dal Makhani

A rich lentil dish made with black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans, simmered slowly with butter, cream, and spices.
